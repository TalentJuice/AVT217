Education

I am a junior graphic design student at George Mason University with a passion for artistic expression and learning. Soon enough I hope to delve into the professional field as a graphic designer for whoever takes me. I think it’ll be a great experience for learning what part of graphic design I love most.

Experience/About Me

Although I have not had the pleasure of working professionally as a graphic designer, my understanding and proficiency of the 3 main adobe applications: Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign continues to grow. Throughout my semesters at George Mason University, I have been honing my skills and breadth of information on these core areas of graphic design. My enjoyment comes from the learning and development each new assignment presents. I’m competitive and my fellow talented classmates continue to push me to strive for better. Here you’ll be able to see my development as an artist as I add new pieces to my portfolio so stay tuned!

My current style is still up in the air but I love color bold typefaces and finding creative ways to manipulate text. I feel my most recent work expresses that most strongly and can be viewed via the link in the home page. Contrary to what you might image my favorite adobe application is InDesign because of its ridged structure and flexible rules helping to create cohesive and complete compositions.
